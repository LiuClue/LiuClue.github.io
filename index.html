Our Mission


CBSS strives to provide value towards a non-traditional Blockchain audience, by advocating the significance of Blockchain technology in all disciplines. Our target audience encompasses STEM related majors, arts and social sciences and beyond, through interdisciplinary educational opportunities and industry connections.
